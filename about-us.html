About Us




Philippine Hamster Keepers started last August 10, 2018 in response to the need of proper information about hamster keeping. Before PHK, there was a group named Mandaluyong Hamster Keepers (MHK) that started March 2017. From MHK, we transitioned to PHK because we only not want to be in a specific area but rather in the whole country, hence the name Philippine Hamster Keepers.





Philippine Hamster Keepers is a Community where new and old owners discuss proper hamster keeping. We aid those who needs help by providing information and real time help. PHK meets regularly for scheduled events such as Anniversary and Christmas party. We try our best to come up with a healthy community. We like to treat ourselves as a family. Our main mission is to educate about proper hamster care and always lean towards improving the quality life of our hamsters.

Mission

Vision

PHK exists to promote proper hamster care in all species in the Philippines by providing the proper guidelines on how to properly care for them.

PHK envisions a future where hamsters are properly and ethically taken care off. Hamsters are not just pets but we see them as part of our family, and they too require specific care. PHK provides a community that supports other keepers and the public in terms of proper care through education and support. PHK hopes that through this movement, current and future hamster owners will strive to give the best care and promote good practice.
